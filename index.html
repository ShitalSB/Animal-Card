Lion

A male lion’s roar can be heard from up to five miles away – the loudest roar of any big cat species.

  • Scientific name: Panthera leo
  • Avarage Length: 2 m(male), 1.6 m(female)
  • Avarage Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Eat Large animals

A lioness has a gestation period of about 110 days, after which she gives birth to up to four cubs. Lions are actually pretty lazy, and spend 16 - 20 hours of the day either resting or sleeping.
